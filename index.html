Game: Prize = $100 × (heads / flips). You choose when to stop between flip 1 and flip 100.

Method: Backward induction — identical to American option pricing. At each node compare stopping value vs continuation value. Walk back from the last flip to flip 0.

Key insight: Always stop after first flip if heads (100% = $100 max). Always continue after tails (0% = $0 < any continuation value).

Threshold: Near the end you only need slightly above 50% to stop. Early on you need a much higher proportion — more flips means more chances to improve.
